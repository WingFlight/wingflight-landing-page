About Wingflight

Wingflight is an open source flight stabilizer built on the proven foundation of Rotorflight, but designed for fixed-wing aircraft. We bring the same engineering excellence, community-driven development, and passion to winged flight. Whether you're flying gliders, 3D sport aircraft, or jets, Wingflight provides rock-solid stabilization and intuitive control.

Our mission is to democratize advanced flight control technology. Built by engineers and pilots, Wingflight combines cutting-edge algorithms with ease-of-use, making professional-grade flight stabilization accessible to the entire community. Join us in shaping the future of open source flight control.

🪂 For Gliders

Stable thermaling and long-distance flight with wind-aware stabilization modes.

🎯 For 3D Aircraft

Full authority control for extreme aerobatics with precision stabilization.

⚡ For Jets & Beyond

High-speed flight control with advanced stabilization and precision systems.
